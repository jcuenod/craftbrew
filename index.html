Help Brew!

Don't worry, this whole thing is designed to be super easy.
Even so, here are some things to keep in mind.

  • Usage: Type whatever you want; the system should automagically guess what you want. If it gets the guess wrong, hit the same letter again and you will loop through the alternate options.
  • Font: We're using unicode (which is good) but you'll need a font that supports it - we recommend SBL Hebrew.
  • Final Forms: When you hit space, they will be converted automatically.
  • Dagesh: It's easy, just use the fullstop.
  • Shuruq: The right way to insert a shuruq is by using a vav/waw and adding a dagesh.
  • Maqaf: Use the minus/dash key.

A Note On Consonants: Sometimes you may want a consonant but a vowel will appear. Hitting tab after the vowel appears will promote the vowel to a consonant. You can cycle through the consonants with tab.
